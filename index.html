About Our Roleplay

Gobal Street Roleplay (GSRP) is a city full of opportunities. Whether you’re a police officer maintaining law and order, a mechanic fixing rides, or a businessman running your empire — the story is yours to write.

We focus on fair gameplay, player-driven economy, and custom systems that create a dynamic environment for both new and veteran players. Our mission is to deliver the best RP experience on SAMP mobile and PC.
